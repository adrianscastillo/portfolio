National Media Office

User Experience and Website Visual Design for National Media Office of the United Arab Emirates

The National Media Office is the UAE's federal agency in charge of coordinating media strategies for the government, shaping how the UAE tells its story at home and abroad.
The design was kept simple and minimal so the content could take the lead. The palette was inspired by the UAE landscape, bringing in warm, natural tones.

Sheikh Zayed

User Experience, Website Visual Design, and Interaction for Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan Website

The Sheikh Zayed website honors the legacy of the UAE's founding father through an immersive digital experience that reflects his vision, values, and contributions to the nation.
The design approach focused on creating an elegant and engaging digital experience that would honor Sheikh Zayed's legacy while making his story accessible to future generations.

An interactive particle system inspired by the sands of the Emirates was used as the main visual element to create a dynamic and immersive experience that reflects the forward-thinking vision of the UAE's founding father.
The story of Sheikh Zayed was brought to life through a series of modules, each designed with a clear purpose. A family tree traced his lineage, timelines highlighted milestones from his life, and maps illustrated the breadth of his influence across and beyond the UAE.
